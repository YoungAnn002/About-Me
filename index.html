I don't really play anymore because I'm more interested in other things and I don't really have time to play. I used to be pretty good though.

I also really enjoy animating. I used to be a lot more into it but I decided that it's not what I wanted to do but I still have fun bringing those stories to life. I've made a music video and a few other fun short videos. I just liked being able to create a story and create a way for others to enjoy my imagination come to life.

Lastly something I really enjoy doing is programming. I mean if I didn't I wouldn't be here right? I think it's fun to be able to problem solve and search for answers to problems. It can be annoying but I still enjoy it no matter what gets thrown at me because it's so rewarding to really figure out something that needed to be figured out. I don't enjoy debugging at all but I think it's fun creating a working solution to a problem.
